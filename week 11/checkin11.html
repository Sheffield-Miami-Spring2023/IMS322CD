P5 Poem Wireframe

Procedure

  1. First, read the description for Project 1: P5 Poem.
  2. Create a wireframe sketch for at least 2 scenes of your P5 Poem. This can be handrawn and photographed or exported as an image file from Figma, Illustrator, or whatever drawing/sketching software you are most comfortable with.
  3. Your wireframe should include the overall layout of the content, interactive behaviors, and transitions. It should also indicate which text will accompany each scene.

Submission

  • Post a reply to this Canvas discussion with the follwing:
    • Attach your wireframe image using the "Images" button in the Canvas text editor.

Grading

  • Full credit will be awarded for all submissions that attempt all steps.
  • Your submission will be viewed in small groups at the next class meeting.

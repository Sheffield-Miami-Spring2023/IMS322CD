Introduce Yourself: Make an introduction page (from scratch)

Objectives

  • Review common HTML elements, including links and images
  • Review CSS selectors and basic styling
  • Introduce yourself to your instructor and classmates

Procedure

  • Create an HTML and a CSS document using a familiar text editor or IDE workflow (e.g. whatever you used in IMS222, Brackets, Notepad++, VS Code, etc.)
  • Add the following to your HTML:
    • Your first and last name in an h1 tag
    • A photo of yourself in an image tag
    • A link to your personal website OR one of your favorite technology or media websites
    • An UNORDERED list with the following information:
      • Your major at Miami
      • 1-3 of your specializations or hobbies
      • Your hometown
  • Style with CSS as follows:
    • Change the background color of the document body
    • Change the background color of the element with your name (something that compliments the body background color)
    • Change the font family of the unordered list
    • Ensure that your photo retains its original aspect ratio and is sized appropriately

Submission

  • Attach your HTML, CSS, and photo files to this Canvas assignment

Grading

  • This first check-in is intended to review and evaluate the class' collective prior experience - full credit will be awarded for all submissions that attempt all steps
  • Your submission will be viewed in-class at the next class meeting

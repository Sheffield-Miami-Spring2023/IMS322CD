Bouncing Ball

A "bouncing ball" animation in p5.

Objectives

  • Getting started with p5.
  • Using basic p5 drawing commands.
  • Changing values between frames in p5.

Procedure

  1. Find the Week 10 assignment in your Team (class section) on Replit and click "Start project" to begin.
  2. Draw a circle at the top of the p5 canvas (centered horizontally).
  3. Make the circle descend to the bottom of the canvas.
  4. When the circle reaches the bottom of the canvas, reverse direction so that it ascends to the top of the canvas.
  5. When the circle reaches the top of the canvas, reverse direction again so that it descends to the bottom. You should now have a p5 sketch with a "ball" that "bounces" continuously from the top to the bottom of the canvas.
  6. Remember to click the green "Run" button as needed to refresh the browser preview update on the right side of the window.

Submission

  1. Click the "Submit" button in the upper-right corner when you are finished. This will notify the instructor of your submission time. You may click "Resubmit" as many times as you'd like to submit changes/updates - however, keep in mind that this will also change the timestamp on your submission.
  2. Submit (copy-paste) the URL of your assignment's Repl (from the address bar) to this Canvas assignment. This will ensure that it gets marked off of your Canvas to-do list.

Post Your Poem

Procedure

  1. First, read the description for Project 1: P5 Poem.
  2. Find a poem that you would like to use as the basis for your project. Remember, this can be a short poem, an excerpt from a longer poem, or your own original poetry (but NO song lyrics). If you're having trouble finding source text, here are a few searchable collections:
    • Poetry Foundation
    • Poets.org
    • Poem Hunter
  3. Your choice should consider the objectives of the project - think about how you might create visuals, sounds, and/or interactive components to accompany and enhance the text.

Submission

  • Post a reply to this Canvas discussion with the following:
    • The text of your selected poem or poem excerpt (post only the portion that you intend to use).
    • The author of your selected poem or poem excerpt.
    • The name of the collection in which your poem appears (if applicable).
    • A link to an online source for your text (if available).

Grading

  • Full credit will be awarded for all submissions that attempt all steps.

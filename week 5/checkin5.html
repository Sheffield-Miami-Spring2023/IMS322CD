Wireframe for the Music Player

Procedure

  1. First, read the description for Week 5 Assignment: Music Player.
  2. Decide on the song and artist/group that you would like to work with, then think about what your music player might look like. What is the color scheme? Where will you put the thumbnail image? How will the buttons be laid out?
  3. If you need references, take a look at how the music players on these sites are designed:
    • SoundCloud
    • Bandcamp
    • YouTube Music
  4. Create a wireframe sketch for your music player. This can be handrawn and photographed or exported as an image file from Figma, Illustrator, or whatever drawing/sketching software you are most comfortable with.
  5. Your wireframe should include some depiction of the intended hover behavior (can be a separate drawing if desired).

Submission

  • Post a reply to this Canvas discussion with the follwing:
    • Attach your wireframe image using the "Images" button in the Canvas text editor.
    • Write 1-2 sentences describing what you expect will be the biggest challenge for you on the Week 5 Assignment.
    • If it is not already evident from your wireframe, please indicate exactly what song/artist/group you are referencing for this assignment.

Grading

  • Full credit will be awarded for all submissions that attempt all steps.
  • Your submission will help the instructor plan content for the next class meeting.

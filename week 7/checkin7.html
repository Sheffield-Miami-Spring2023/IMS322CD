Wireframe for the Data Viz

Procedure

  1. First, read the description for Week 7 Assignment: Data Viz.
  2. Decide on how you will graphically visualize the pet data (e.g. vertical or horizontal bar graph, pie chart, square proportional area chart). Also give consideration to the overall layout of the other text-based representations of data.
  3. Create a wireframe sketch for your data visualization results. This can be handrawn and photographed or exported as an image file from Figma, Illustrator, or whatever drawing/sketching software you are most comfortable with.
  4. Your wireframe should include both desktop and mobile layouts.

Submission

  • Post a reply to this Canvas discussion with the follwing:
    • Attach your wireframe image using the "Images" button in the Canvas text editor.
    • Write 1-2 sentences describing what you expect will be the biggest challenge for you on the Week 7 Assignment.

Grading

  • Full credit will be awarded for all submissions that attempt all steps.
  • Your submission will help the instructor plan content for the next class meeting.

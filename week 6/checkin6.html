Wireframe for the Profile Questionnaire

Procedure

  1. First, read the description for Week 6 Assignment: Profile Questionnaire.
  2. Decide on your community's theme and the questions that you'll be asking to gather user profile information. Then, think about what your profile quesionnaire and resulting bio might look like. What is the color scheme? Where will you put the avatar image? How will the select and entry controls be laid out?
  3. If you need references, take a look at how the bios on sites like Twitter, Instagram, Tinder, and other social websites are designed. What kind of information do they include? How is the different information presented?
  4. Create a wireframe sketch for your profile quesionnaire and bio. This can be handrawn and photographed or exported as an image file from Figma, Illustrator, or whatever drawing/sketching software you are most comfortable with.
  5. Your wireframe should include both desktop and mobile layouts for both the quesionnaire and bio portions.

Submission

  • Post a reply to this Canvas discussion with the follwing:
    • Attach your wireframe image using the "Images" button in the Canvas text editor.
    • Write 1-2 sentences describing what you expect will be the biggest challenge for you on the Week 6 Assignment.
    • If it is not already evident from your wireframe, please indicate exactly what your community is for this assignment.

Grading

  • Full credit will be awarded for all submissions that attempt all steps.
  • Your submission will help the instructor plan content for the next class meeting.

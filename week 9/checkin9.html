Wireframe for the DIY Carousel

Procedure

  1. First, read the description for Week 9 Assignment: DIY Carousel.
  2. Decide on your carousel layout. Will this look like a typcical left-to-right slideshow, or something different? How will users navigate the content sections and how will the controls be laid out? How will the carousel transision between different sections?
  3. Create a wireframe sketch for your DIY Carousel. This can be handrawn and photographed or exported as an image file from Figma, Illustrator, or whatever drawing/sketching software you are most comfortable with.
  4. Your wireframe should include the overall layout of the content and controls, control behaviors (e.g. hover, active indicator buttons, etc.), and content transition behaviors.
  5. OPTIONAL: You can optionlly choose a theme and design your carousel to suit the content theme (e.g. using highway exit arrows for buttons with a roadtrip theme). If you do, please indicate this in your wireframe. Otherwise, placeholder images and text is acceptable for your wireframe and final submission.

Submission

    • Attach your wireframe image using the "Images" button in the Canvas text editor.
    • Write 1-2 sentences describing what you expect will be the biggest challenge for you on the Week 9 Assignment.

Grading

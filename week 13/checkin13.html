My Gallery Wireframe

Procedure

  1. First, read the description for Project 2: P5 My Gallery.
  2. Create a wireframe sketch for at your gallery. This can be handrawn and photographed or exported as an image file from Figma, Illustrator, or whatever drawing/sketching software you are most comfortable with.
  3. Your wireframe should include the overall layout of the content and navigation behaviors. It should also indicate which previous assignments you intend to include in your gallery.

Submission

  • Post a reply to this Canvas discussion with the follwing:
    • Attach your wireframe image using the "Images" button in the Canvas text editor.

Grading

  • Full credit will be awarded for all submissions that attempt all steps.
  • Your submission will be viewed in small groups at the next class meeting.

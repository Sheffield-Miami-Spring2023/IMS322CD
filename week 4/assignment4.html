Pop-Up Unit Converter

A quick temperature conversion.

Objectives

  • Practice working with simple user input and JavaScript operators.

Procedure

  1. Find the Week 4 assignment in your Team (class section) on Replit and click "Start project" to begin.
  2. The script.js file is already set up to accept user input and display output to the HTML body. You job is to apply arithmetic operations to the provided variables in order to convert Fahrenheit to Celsius and Kelvin.
    • Celsius = Fahrenheit-32 * 5/9
    • Kelvin = Celsius + 273.15
    • PEMDAS rules apply in JavaScript! You may need this for one of the conversions...
  3. Remember to click the green "Run" button as needed to refresh the browser preview update on the right side of the window.

Submission

  1. Click the "Submit" button in the upper-right corner when you are finished. This will notify the instructor of your submission time. You may click "Resubmit" as many times as you'd like to submit changes/updates - however, keep in mind that this will also change the timestamp on your submission.
  2. Submit (copy-paste) the URL of your assignment's Repl (from the address bar) to this Canvas assignment. This will ensure that it gets marked off of your Canvas to-do list.

Grading

  • One point will be deducted for each error.
